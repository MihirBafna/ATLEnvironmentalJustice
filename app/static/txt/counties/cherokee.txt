Located in the northern part of Atlanta, Cherokee county has recently experienced some growth, with a 200 percent population surge between 2000 and 2010. So, with more people in the county there is more focus on the county’s waste treatment system, which has been mistreated by the city of Canton’s Water Pollution Control Plant. The levels of phosphorus, nitrogen, and other chemicals exceeded the acceptable permit limit, which negatively impacted the city’s water supply. This area is home to financially disadvantaged individuals and 20% of its residents are minorities.
