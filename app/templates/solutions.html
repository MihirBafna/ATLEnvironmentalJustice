 What To Do?

It's one thing to learn and be informed about the injustice, but we need action for actual change. We urge you to get involved, whether that be through social media, the government, or non-profit organizations. We have listed some ways you can help make a difference and combat the environmental racism we see today. Click below to explore.

Explore Your Options

Through the Government

Government is one way to create change and combat environmental injustice. Use your voice to elect the officials at the local, state, and federal level that will best help your community fight climate change and environmental justice. Write to your elected officials and let them know what policies you care about and how they can help your community. Below is a list of your local and state elected government officials.

Through Organizations

Activism through non-profit organizations allow our society to get one step closer to environmental justice. We have provided a list of non-profit organizations that are fighting environmental injustice for you here. Please join, support, or donate to these organizations in order to create cleaner, healthier neighborhoods both here in Atlanta and in cities across the country. The link below also contains some other great links to environmental organizations based in Georgia.
