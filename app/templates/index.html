ABOUT THE
INJUSTICE


Never heard about environmental injustice?

Well, it is a simple concept: if you look at regions of lower socioeconomic status, you will find that they have worse environmental qualities and thus a lowered quality of life. Unfortunately, these poorer areas are mainly home to minority groups-those that fray away from the "mythical norm"(Lorde). This injustice is not a random occurrence. It is an act of environmental racism.
